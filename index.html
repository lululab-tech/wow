Frequently asked questions
How do I import my existing CV?
Go to CVs in the sidebar and click New CV. You can drag and drop a PDF or DOCX file, or paste text directly. WOW will parse your existing CV and make it editable.
How does drag-and-drop work on the Kanban board?
Click and hold any job card on the Kanban board or dashboard pipeline, then drag it into a different column. Dropping it there instantly updates the job's status. This works on both the mini pipeline on the dashboard and the full Kanban view.
How does AI CV tailoring work?
In the CV editor sidebar, click ✦ Tailor for [company]. This sends your CV and the job description to Claude AI, which highlights missing keywords, suggests restructuring, and rewrites sections to better match the role. Your original CV is never overwritten.
What can the AI Assistant do?
The AI Assistant has four modes: CV Feedback (review and improve your CV), Cover Letter (draft tailored letters), Interview Prep (generate questions and model answers), and Job Match (score your fit against a job description). All powered by Claude AI.
What's included in the Free plan?
The Free plan includes up to 3 CVs, 5 cover letters, 50 AI messages per month, unlimited job tracking, and basic interview prep. Pro (£9/month) gives you unlimited everything, ATS scoring, and priority support.
How do I export or delete my data?
Go to Account → Data & Privacy. You can export a ZIP of all your CVs, cover letters and job data at any time. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, click Delete account in the danger zone.
Can I share my CV or cover letter with a recruiter?
Yes — click the Share button on any CV or cover letter to generate a view-only link. Links expire after 30 days by default, but you can revoke access at any time from the Share modal.
How does interview prep work?
Go to Interview Prep to see prep cards for each upcoming interview. Click Prepare → to open the AI coach, which generates role-specific questions and gives you real-time feedback on your practice answers. Progress is tracked per interview.
Still need help?
Our support team typically responds within 4 hours on weekdays.
